She Started Off Her Modeling Career While Still a Teenager

Jordyn Blum started her career as a model while still a teenager and was already famous at the age of 17. Rightly put, Jordyn knew early enough what she wanted to make out of her life. She began modeling in her late teens and by 1994 when she was about 18 years old, she was featured on the cover of Teen Magazine.

With her beautiful physique, Jordyn did well enough to attract several reputable brands to sign deals as their brand ambassador in the United States and she continued modeling into her early twenties before she sought a career change in the entertainment industry as a film director.

She Directed Her First Film in the Year 2002

Fresh on the job, Jordyn directed her first short film titled Foo Fighters: Walking a Line in 2002 alongside Chris Osterhus as co-director. The short film featured members of the Foo Fighters music band – Dave Grohl and Taylor Hawkins.

Next in her film directing portfolio, she was scheduled to direct a movie about the Foo Fighters but for reasons that have not been publicly disclosed, the project never saw the light of the day. She was then cast in The GQ Men of the Year Awards in 2003 alongside Adrien Brody and Monica Belluci.

Summarily, Jordyn Blum has only one film directorial credit for now but that does not make her less of a film director when compared to others with a long list of work in their portfolios. Jordy gives more of her attention to raising her kids, supporting her husband, and running their family in general.

David Grohl and Jordyn Blum Met for the First Time in 2001

Reports had it that he scribbled the words “You’re my future wife” on a piece of paper he passed across to her. Dave also did get Jordyn drunk on their first meeting and their acquaintance which started in a comical manner blossomed into a relationship.

He even at a time cut off communication with Jordyn but he soon had a turning point when his bandmate Taylor Hawkins suffered a drug overdose. It was at this point that Dave sought to get things right in his life and thank goodness, Jordyn was right there to help him through.

Jordyn Blum Has Been David Grohl’s Wife For 20 Years and They are Blessed With 3 Children

The marriage betweeen David and Jordyn has lasted for 20 years and still counting. They have remained married since then and so far, their union has been an epitome of what a celebrity marriage should be, especially in Hollywood; an industry fraught with marriage scandals and rife with divorces.

They had their first child, a baby girl they named Violet Maye Grohl sometime in 2006. Harper Willow Grohl, their second child, also a girl, came in 2009 while their youngest child, another girl named Ophelia Saint Grohl, was born precisely on the 8th of August 2014.

Violet is aged 17 while Harper is 14 years old. The youngest Grohl child Ophelia is 5 years old.
