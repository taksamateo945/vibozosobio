Top 10 Best Shoe Brands In The World!

 Posted on August 5, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  1033 words  |  Larita Shotwell

If you want to look more attractive with your entire attire, then a grandeur collection of shoes is what adds charms to your personality. The shoe industries have globally endured double quick to a massive market over the decades. The footwear market comes out to be a tremendous retail revolution in the industrial sector. When it comes to looks and dressing gestures, one of the first names that strike your mind is shoes. 

“Shoe defines your personality” is an appropriate saying when it comes to your sense in fashion. Today where the trend is an excellent extension in this era, shoe likers are everywhere. What will be the initial thing that comes into your mind when you come forward to purchase a new pair of shoes? Shape, size, color, material, durability, comfort, and, most importantly, the Brands!

Yes! Brands are the most vital characteristic of a shoe. A good brand stays longer and makes you feel more confident. Here we are for you with a list of Top 10 best shoe brands in the world highly admired for their highest sales and flourishing quality.

1. Nike

Nike is one of the renowned largest shoe brands across the world. American Origin Corporation founded it in 1964, and Phil knight and Bill Bowerman are its co-founders. This brand always comes with innovation, technology, and attractive marketing campaigns promoting various celebrity endorsements like Ronaldo, Neymar, and Tiger Woods. For its awesome styles and durability of both casual and sports shoes, this billion-dollar International shoe brand is renowned for best footwear. Nike operates its whole market via headquarters located in Washington Country in the US.

2. Adidas

Adidas is a popular German origin company that was founded by Adolf Dassler in 1924. By using advanced technology, it offers so stylish, flexible, and ultra-comfy shoes. His headquarter is located in Herzogenaurach in Germany through which this billion-dollar brand is operated throughout the world. Along with footwear, this company associates with other related items too. It also sponsors significant events like the UEFA Champions League and New York Yankees.

3. Jordan

Jordan is a brand that provides you a comfortable pair of shoes. This brand was founded in 1984 by American Origin Company as being a child brand of Nike. Michael Jordan is a founder of this well-renowned brand. This brand is also responsible for the manufacturing of basketball shoes. Many well-known celebrities like Carmelo Anthony, Jimmy Butler, Chris Paul, and Blake Griffin have sponsorship deals with this high-profile brand. WNBA, NASCAR, NFL, and MLB are some sponsored leagues of this brand.

4. Reebok

This renowned and innovative brand was founded in 1958 by Jeff and Joe Foster. It is well renowned for its traditional footwear. This brand is a child brand of Adidas that steps in itself in the list of best shoe brands across the world. This high-profile brand deals in producing, designing, selling, and distribution of both casual and sportswear. This brand is responsible for sponsoring kits for CSK and KKR during the IPL season in India.

5. Kering (PUMA)

PUMA brand is a German origin company founded by Rudolf Dassler in 1948. This brand widely spreads its distributors in all-around 120 countries and operates its marketing via its headquarters in Herzogenaurach in Germany. PUMA is a well-known brand known for its high-profile comfort, durability, and emulous designs with styles. Its footwear offers products in various sports like football, motorsports, golf, basketballs, etc. Many big celebrities like Boris Becker, Virat Kohli, and Martina Navratilova endure its sponsorship deals due to its colossal profile.  

6. Skechers

Skechers is one of the billion-dollar companies that raise new heights in the shoe market. Robert Greenberg founded this brand in 1992. In the American market, this brand is being on the list of the nation’s top three most significant brands. Lifestyle and performance footwears lie in terms of this brand. This renowned brand has approx 900 styles and esteemed designs for its variety of customers. Many celebrities like Camilo Cabello, Kelly Brook, and Brook Henderson are all associates with its sponsorship deals. 

7. Fila

Fila is known for its iconic designs of various products. Fila as stands by Fabbrica Italiana Lapis ed Affin. Johan Fila founded it in 1911. Its headquarters is located in South Korea. This brand has diverse customise styles and a comfy pair of designs. This brand begins as a textile firm in Biella, Italy. Casual footwear, sports wears, and activewear are the primary and widely spread product of this brand. This high-profile brand has a good relationship with various spots categories like soccer, tennis, basketball, and many more.

8. Bata

Bata is a renowned brand for more than 100 years in the world of the shoe industry. It was founded in August 1894 by Tomas Bata in the Czech Republic. Now in 2020, Bata is led by its third generation. This luxurious brand provides a blend of comfy and fashionable products. Their distributors spread in over 60 countries, along with E-commerce. It includes various casual and formal shoes, belts, bags, and other accessories to every age group. If we look through the company’s turnover, we find it had around 28,900 million turnovers in the financial year 2018-2019.

9. New Balance

This Boston-based multinational brand is an American origin company founded in 1906 by William J Riley. It is one of the largest manufacturer companies in the shoe market. Being a premium brand, it offers diverse style, comfort, athletic and casual footwear. The company uses integrated technologies with many innovative ideas. Besides casual footwear, this brand is associated with sports like soccer, basketball, tennis, golf, and many more. Many prominent personalities like Dale Steyn, Matt Reid, Trayvon Bromell, and Mathew Wade bind several sponsorship deals with New Balance.

10. Vans

Vans is a renowned shoe brand founded in 1966 by James Van Doren, Gordon C Lee, and Paul van Doren. It operates as a child company of VF Corporation. This brand is an American origin company that has a wide variety of manufacturing skateboarding shoes. The main targets of this brand are elite athletes for the taste of the younger generation. Its E-commerce service provides customization of shoe designs by the customer on the online platform.
