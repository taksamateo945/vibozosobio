In addition to all this, Beckham has shown a keen interest in photography and has successfully launched a career in that field as well. Now moving towards his romantic life, Brooklyn Beckham’s name has been linked with many celebrities over the years including actresses Chloe Grace Moretz and Sonia Ben Ammar as well as models Sofia Richie, Lexi Wood, and Hana Cross.
