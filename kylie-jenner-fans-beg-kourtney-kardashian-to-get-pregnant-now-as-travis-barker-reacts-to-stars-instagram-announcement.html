KYLIE Jenner fans are now wanting to see Kourtney Kardashian “get pregnant” following the reality star’s surprising Instagram announcement.

Kourtney as well as her boyfriend Travis Barker were quick to respond to the makeup mogul’s exciting news.

After Kylie, 24, revealed to the world she is expecting her second child with Travis Scott, plenty of her family and friends were quick to respond.

Kourtney wrote: “Crying, this is so beautiful – my blessed angel sister.”

However, the Blink-182 drummer also reacted, as he shared a praying hands and heart emojis.

Fans noticed the couple’s comments and took this time to encourage both Kourtney, 42, and Travis, 45, to have a baby next.

One person wrote: “Kourtney and you next, please!” while another chimed in, “You and your Travis are next at this point.”

A third fan added: “You’re next!”

Travis and Kourtney, who were recently on an Italian getaway, have had fans on the lookout for baby clues.

However, the Keeping Up With The Kardashians alum hasn’t shutdown the rumors, as she instead added fuel to the fire when she documented herself receiving a delivery from Buy Buy Baby.

She also previously shared a post about gaining weight “not because of food,” has been wearing loose clothes, given up eating meat and fish, and stopped drinking alcohol.

KOURTNEY & SCOTT’S DRAMA

Kourtney shares three kids with ex Scott Disick, however, he isn’t too happy seeing his baby mama with the musician, especially with their PDA sessions.

The father of three even allegedly messaged Kourtney’s other ex, model Younes Bendjima in hopes of trolling her together – however, was quickly rejected.

His alleged attempt to bash Kourtney was also leaked, leaving Scott feeling “mortified.”

An insider told E! News: “He had a low moment and was looking to someone who could relate to his feelings, and immediately regretted sending that message.”

The source also claimed that, “Scott is still very bitter about Kourtney and Travis’ relationship…”

Scott, who just parted ways with girlfriend Amelia Hamlin, saw his relationship with Kourtney end in 2015.

SOME GOOD NEWS!

A source told The Sun: “The entire family is beyond excited. They truly are a family of ‘the more the merrier’ so the more they can add, they welcome that.

“Kylie’s always wanted to add more kids, that’s no secret so this is a really happy blessing.”
