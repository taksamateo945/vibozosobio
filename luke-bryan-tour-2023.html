Luke Bryans Setlist for the 2023 Country On Tour Revealed: Get Ready for an Unforgettable Show

 Posted on August 3, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  705 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Luke Bryan’s 2023 Country On Tour has begun! This past weekend, the American country singer, songwriter, and television personality performed three gigs to launch his 2023 Country On Tour.

This time, the American Idol judge, 46, will offer talented musicians a fantastic opportunity to shine throughout the tour. Learn more about Luke Bryan’s 2023 Country On Tour setlist by scrolling down further.

Luke Bryan has kicked off ‘2023 Country On Tour’

Yes, it is what you read. On June 15 in Syracuse, New York, the That’s My Kind of Night singer kicked off his 2023 Country On Tour. Then, on June 16 and June 17, he gave performances in Darien Centre, New York, and Toronto, Ontario, respectively.

Among the many cities the country music artist will visit on his tour are Philadelphia, Wantagh, Holmdel, Columbia, Hershey, Portland, Wheatland, Denver, and Salt Lake City.

Luke Bryan’s ‘2023 Country On Tour’ will feature newcomers

Rising stars including Conner Smith, Alana Springsteen, Hailey Whitters, Dean, Jon Langston, Tyler Braden, Chase, and Ashley Cooke will accompany Bryan on his 2023 Country On Tour.

Luke stated in a statement, “Artists enter the industry to create music and perform it for the public. For me, it’s all about giving it my all on that stage. This year, I’m thrilled to be able to support and ride along with so many brilliant new artists. One of the most fulfilling aspects of our work is it.
